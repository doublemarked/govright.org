GovRight Accepted as a Member to the Free Access to Law Movement

by Andrew Marino | May 30, 2016 | Uncategorized | 0 comments

We are pleased to announce that GovRight has been accepted as a member of FALM – the Free Access to Law Movement. FALM is a voluntary alliance of more than 50 organizations from around the world. FALM members provide and support free access to legal information, consistent with the principles of the Free Access to Law Movement. Members support and aspire to uphold the organization’s Declaration on Free Access to Law.

GovRight will be represented by its co-founders, Tarik Nesh-Nash and Heath Morrison.
Tarik and Heath would like to thank the following people and organizations for nominating GovRight as a FALM member: Tom Bruce of the Legal Information Institute at Cornell University, Pompeu Casanovas from the Institute of Law and Technology, Barcelona as well as the Australasian Legal Information Institute.

On behalf of the entire GovRight team, we are pleased to be able to work and connect with this respected group of colleagues.
