Opportunity: Admin Assistant (Part-time, Kyiv)

To apply for this position, please send your CV and cover letter to admin@govright.org.

GovRight is seeking a part-time administrative assistant to aid in the establishment and growth of GovRight’s operations in Kyiv, Ukraine. This role will start part-time but may grow into a full time position.

Duties

You will assist our local CTO in the establishment of a small local office (4-6 people) and in performing business development in Ukraine. This includes some or all of the following:

  • Assisting in the search for office space, and dealing with paperwork/logistics of the office.
  • Handling small issues with our employees/contractors (payments, talking to the accountant, job postings, etc.)
  • Researching potential local partners, reaching out to similar organizations, etc.

Qualifications

The ideal candidate shall:

  • Have a general understanding of business operations in Ukraine.
  • Be resourceful, willing and able to reach out to experts to accomplish a task.
  • Speak and write English at least on an Intermediate level.
  • Be motivated, confident, and not lazy.
  • Be familiar and active with modern technology and social media (have a smart phone, use Facebook, etc.)

Working with GovRight

GovRight is an organization committed to increasing meaningful collaboration between citizens and government. We have a particular focus on legislation and the technology surrounding participation, an expertise built through years of activity in civil society, business, and academia in the United States, MENA region, and Eastern Europe. Our work is highly international and has received broad attention and recognition.

We are supported by a number of leading donors and partner organizations, including USAID, EED, The US Institute for Peace, the World Resources Institute, Cornell University, and Mundiapolis University.

To apply for this position, please send your CV and cover letter to admin@govright.org.
