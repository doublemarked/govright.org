Legislation Lab: Law Discussion Details

Our team is working to process your law document. This involves deconstructing it into components, inserting these into the GovRight legal database, and using it to build the foundation of your law discussion. All very exciting!
In the meantime, we need some additional detailed information in order to finalize the setup and configuration of your law discussion. Please fill out this form as completely as possible. If you need assistance or have any questions, please contact us at contact@govright.org. We’re happy to help.

  • Basic information

  • Law Document Information

  • At the heart of Legislation Lab is an interface for citizens to interact with legal text. Help us understand the nature of the legal text or document you would like to engage the public on.

    All information in this section is intended for publication, and thus should be written with the citizen audience in mind.

    Important: Write in your local language. If you require more than one language please fill out this form a 2nd time with the 2nd language, or send us an email to discuss how to get your localized content published.

  • In which official language or languages is this legislation provided?
  • Please provide several paragraphs or more of text describing the background and purpose of this law document. This text should be factual and is intended to give visitors an understanding of the context and scope of the law. The text will be displayed prominently on your Legislation Lab law discussion.
  • Please provide several paragraphs or more of text describing your subjective analysis of this law. This is is where you may contain your initiative's talking points supporting or opposing the law. To increase the credibility of your law discussion we encourage your analysis to stay focused on what is good and bad about the contents of the law document.
  • Please provide a list of dates and events (one per line) associated with this law and your law discussion initiative. Examples include publication dates (both past and future), upcoming dates associated with your initiative, and more.

  • Site Customization

  • A one to two sentence introduction to your law discussion that aims to inspire participation. For example: 'Parliament is deciding the rights citizens have to government data and what data it may collect. What do citizens want?'
  • A background image to show on the overview page of your law discussion. Must be at least 1400x337 pixels.

  • Your Initiative

  • Please help us understand your organization or movement and the objectives you have for your law discussion. Some of this information will be published on your Legislation Lab law discussion. It will also help us understand how best to enable and support you.
  • Please tell us which languages your audience communicate in.
  • Please provide a several sentence bio about your initiative and its goals.
