About GovRight

GovRight is an organization committed to increasing meaningful collaboration between citizens and government. We have a particular focus on legislation and the technology surrounding participation, an expertise built through years of activity in civil society, business, and academia in the United States, MENA region, and Eastern Europe.

GovRight is a US-based social enterprise with operations in Kyiv, Ukraine and Tangier, Morocco. Our organization has a truly international focus, made possible by strong, ongoing partnerships with local organizations. We provide expertise and technology to our partner organizations that support the partner’s existing mission and objectives of social progress. GovRight has had the pleasure of working with a wide variety of organizations, large and small, in a wide range of countries and contexts.

Objectives



Access to Law

The publication of law documents and data in a format that is modern, accessible, and designed to be shared and integrated.



Civic Education

Applying educational methodology and technology to create citizens that better understand the function and effects of the legislation in their country.



Participation

Creating opportunities for citizens to voice their knowledge and opinions on the purpose and implementation of legislation.
